Map and Design Portfolio

Hi! I am a recent graduate of University of Toronto's Geography Bachelors' Program. My minors were in Urban Studies and Geographic Information Systems (GIS).
I am passionate about applying GIS and spatial statistics to solving urban issues. I particularly enjoy using maps for knowledge sharing and community engagement.
I am currently working with The Beach BIA to create a webmapping tool designed to change the way beachgoers explore their neighbourhood.

Beaches BIA Interactive Web Mapping Tool

Web tool to change how you explore The Beaches
Explore pre-built adventure routes

Working in a small development team to provide a tool for The Beach BIA to increase foot traffic, encourage commercial activity, and bring attention to their park system. 'Take The Scenic Route' is a web tool where users can find curated routes for exploration of the parks, greenspaces, stores, and third spaces in the neighbourhood. Launching soon on The Beach BIA main website.
